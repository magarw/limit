Une u dei u Syiem uba sngaid
U Syiem uba sngaid u don ia u ksew uba raikhoh.
U Syiem uba sngaid bad u ksew uba raikhoh ki ia leit iaid kai.
U Ksew u iohi ia ka sim.
U Ksew u beh ia ka sim.
U Syiem u beh nadien jong u ksew.
Ki ia mareh sted bha.
Ki ia mareh kumta shi kumta bun bun sngi.
U Syiem u lap ia u Ksew.
Mynta u Syiem uba sngaid u lah raikhoh.
